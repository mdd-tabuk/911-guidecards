911 Dispatcher Guidecards

General Emergency Questions

  • What is the location of the emergency?
  • What is your phone number?
  • Can you describe what happened?
  • Is anyone injured?
  • Are there weapons involved?

Medical Emergency Questions

  • Is the patient conscious and breathing?
  • What are the symptoms?
  • How old is the patient?
  • Is there any severe bleeding?
  • Do you know if the patient has any medical history?

Fire Emergency Questions

  • What is on fire?
  • Is anyone trapped?
  • Is there smoke or flames visible?
  • Are hazardous materials involved?
  • Have you evacuated the area?

Police Emergency Questions

  • What is the suspect's description?
  • Are they armed?
  • What direction did they go?
  • Are there any injuries?
  • Is the suspect still on scene?
