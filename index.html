ALL CALLER INTERROGATION (ACI)

Where is the Patient? (Address or Location)

What is your call back number and your name?

What is the problem?

Is the patient conscious? (Able to talk)

No: Continue

  • Describe the patient’s breathing.
  • If breathing is not Agonal, go to Unconscious / Syncope.
  • If breathing is Agonal, dispatch the ambulance.
  • Ask: "Do you want to do CPR? I'll help you."
  • YES: Go to CPR (Adult / Child / Infant).
  • NO: "Help is on the way. Stay on the line."

YES: Determine age, sex, and chief complaint.

  • Proceed to the appropriate guidecard.
  • Check patient’s condition.
  • Stay on the line for further details.
